Gallery of Characters

Explore the world of Elites with the list of characters and know their personality and identity.

Kiyotaka Ayanokōji

"I've never once thought of you as an ally. Not you. Not Kushida. Not Hirata. All humans are nothing but tools..."

Kiyotaka Ayanokōji is the main protagonist of the You-Zitsu series. He is currently a second-year student of Advanced Nurturing High School. He was placed in D-Class after scoring precisely 50 points in every subject during the entrance exam.

Suzune Horikita

"If I can't do something, I'll be holding Class D back... I, who looked down my nose at the others..."

Suzune Horikita is a second-year student of Advanced Nurturing High School. She is a member of Class 1-D, despite her academic ability, due to her inability to work with others. She is determined to move to A-Class.

Kei Karuizawa

"In the end, nothing changed even after I came to this school. No, that's wrong... Maybe I never had any intention of changing."

Kei Karuizawa is one of the heroines of the You-Zitsu series. She is a prominent figure in Class D and is considered the leader of the girls in her class.

Kikyō Kushida

"I'm living as my ideal self. I always knew I was blessed even when compared to my peers..."

Kikyō Kushida is a student of Class 1-D and is quite popular due to her friendly nature and desire to befriend everyone.

Arisu Sakayanagi

"I don't want to be interrupted. I'm the only one worthy of burying the false genius."

Arisu Sakayanagi is a prominent character known for her intelligence and confidence. She is often considered one of the most powerful students in the school.

Airi Sakura

"I――from the bottom of my heart, want a person that can reach me."

Airi Sakura is a teenage girl with waist-length pink hair tied in twin pigtails. She is known for her beauty and self-reflection.

Kakeru Ryūen

"This is the way I do things. There can be nothing more or less."

Kakeru Ryūen is the class representative of Class 1-C. He is known for his ruthless leadership style and his desire to dominate the school.

Ken Sudō

"Normally, I dislike playing with others as a team, but miraculously, I was able to make it work in the case of basketball."

Ken Sudō is a student of Class 2-D and a talented athlete. Despite his academic struggles, his athleticism is outstanding.
